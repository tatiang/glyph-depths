📖 How to Play

Objective

Descend 10 floors of the Unnamed Depths and defeat the boss on Floor 10 to escape.

Movement & Combat
MoveD-pad, WASD, or arrow keys. Every move advances one turn.
AttackBump into an enemy to attack it. Enemies act each turn you do.
Wait⏳ button or Space — skip your turn without moving.
Crits10% chance to deal double damage (enemies crit less on early floors).
DodgeBase 5% chance to evade; Rogue class has +15% dodge.
Items
Pick upWalk over items or tap ✋ Get. Auto-pickup is on by default.
Use / EquipTap any slot in the inventory bar at the bottom. Choose Equip, Use, Throw, or Drop.
Quick Use🧪 Use button or U — opens a fast-select for potions & scrolls.
Potions 🧪Unknown until used. Effects are randomized each run.
Scrolls 📜Randomized labels each run. Wizards start with identified scrolls.
Daggers 🗡️Thrown at enemies — enter throw mode then pick a direction.
Cursed ⚠Cursed gear gives bonus stats but locks to your slot. Use a Scroll of Remove Curse to free it.
Hunger
DrainHunger falls over time. At 0 you lose 1 HP every 5 turns.
RestoreEat food rations 🍖 or use a Ring of Hunger to halve drain rate.
BerserkerHunger drains 2× faster — plan food carefully.
Special Tiles
▼ One-WayEntering a one-way door seals it behind you. Bash the sealed door 5 times (–1 HP each) to break through.
✦ GlyphMysterious runes — step on for a random effect. Usually helpful, occasionally harmful.
≡ StairsDescend with 🚪 Stairs button or S. No going back up.
Classes
🧝 AdventurerBalanced. Free Rapid Regeneration. Leather Armor + Healing Potion.
🪖 Berserker22 HP / +5 ATK. Rage grants +3 ATK below 40% HP. Eats 2× food.
🥷 Rogue10 HP. 15% dodge, 20% crit. Starts with 6 Throwing Daggers.
🧙 Wizard11 HP / +1 ATK. Scrolls deal double effect. Starts with 3 identified scrolls.
Level Ups & Perks

Killing enemies gives XP. On level up pick one of three perks. Rare perks include: Glass Cannon (2× ATK, half HP), Vampiric Strikes (heal 20% of damage dealt), Iron Skin (–1 incoming damage), Battle Fury (+3 ATK below 30% HP).

Merchant

Shops on floors 3, 6, and 9. Buy with gold 💰. Tap 🔄 Refresh Stock (25💰, 2 uses) to reroll wares.
